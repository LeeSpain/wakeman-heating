Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our plumbing services

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments. For larger jobs, we typically require a deposit upfront with the balance due upon completion of the work.

Are your plumbers certified and insured?

Yes, all our plumbers are fully certified, experienced professionals. We are fully insured with public liability insurance and professional indemnity insurance for your peace of mind.

How quickly can you respond to a plumbing emergency?

For emergency plumbing issues, we typically respond within 1-2 hours, depending on your location. Our emergency team is available 24/7, including weekends and holidays.

Do you offer warranties on plumbing work?

Yes, all our plumbing work comes with a 12-month warranty on both parts and labor. This covers any issues that might arise from the work we've completed.

How do I know if I have a water leak?

Signs of a water leak include unexplained increases in your water bill, damp spots on walls or ceilings, reduced water pressure, or the sound of running water when no taps are on. If you suspect a leak, it's best to have it checked promptly to prevent water damage.
